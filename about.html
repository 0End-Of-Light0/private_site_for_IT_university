About us

X is a series of sci-fi space trading and combat simulators created by German developer Egosoft. The series is set in the X Universe, where several races inhabit a series of worlds connected by transitional gates. X Series Released in 1999 on the Windows platform, the series consists of five core games: X: Beyond the Frontier, X2: Threat, X3: Reunion, X Rebirth and X4: Foundations. The games in the series are supplemented by sequels that both add features and expand the story. The story can be picked up in all games except X-Tension, where the player takes part in missions as events unfold.
